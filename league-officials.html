Fantasy Football League

League Officials

  • Commissioner

    Responsible for overseeing the entire league, making decisions on disputes, and ensuring the rules are followed.

  • Assistant Commissioner

    Assists the Commissioner and assumes duties in their absence.

  • Treasurer

    Responsible for managing the league's finances, including collecting dues and distributing prizes.

Rules and Governance

  • Rules Committee

    A group of 2-5 members who review and propose changes to the league's rules, including the constitution and bylaws.

  • Constitution Committee

    A group of 2-5 members who review and propose changes to the league's constitution.

  • Bylaws Committee

    A group of 2-5 members who review and propose changes to the league's bylaws.

Player Management

  • Contract Committee

    Responsible for reviewing and approving player contracts, including extensions and trades.

  • Roster Committee

    Responsible for reviewing and approving roster moves, including adds, drops, and trades.

  • Waiver Wire Committee

    Responsible for managing the waiver wire process, including setting waiver wire priorities and resolving waiver wire disputes.

Free Agent Acquisition Budget (FAAB)

  • FAAB Committee

    Responsible for managing the FAAB system, including setting budgets, reviewing bids, and resolving FAAB disputes.

  • FAAB Auditor

    Responsible for reviewing and verifying FAAB bids and transactions.

Auction Draft

  • Auction Draft Committee

    Responsible for organizing and conducting the auction draft, including setting draft order and rules.

  • Auction Draft Commissioner

    Responsible for overseeing the auction draft process and resolving disputes.

Scoring and Statistics

  • Scoring Committee

    Responsible for reviewing and updating the league's unique scoring system, including making adjustments as needed.

  • Statistics Committee

    Responsible for reviewing and verifying team and player statistics.

Dispute Resolution

  • Dispute Resolution Committee

    A group of 2-5 members who review and resolve disputes between teams, including protests and appeals.

  • Appeals Committee

    A group of 2-5 members who review and resolve appeals of dispute resolution decisions.

Communication and Technology

  • Webmaster

    Responsible for managing the league's website and online presence.

  • Forum Moderator

    Responsible for managing the league's online forum and ensuring that discussions remain respectful and on-topic.
