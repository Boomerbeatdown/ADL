Waivers

FAAB

Each team has a FAAB budget of $100 to use for the entire season.

The FAAB budget is used to bid on free agents and waiver wire players.

The team with the highest bid on a player will be awarded that player.

Your current FAAB budget: $100

Waiver Priority

The team owner with the highest FAAB bid on a player will receive the player when waivers clear.

If multiple teams bid the same amount, the team with the better waiver priority (closest to "1") will receive the player.

Free Agents

All available players not on waivers are considered free agents.

Players dropped by an owner reside on waivers for at least 48 hours before clearing waivers.

At the clearance time, the player is assigned to either a team (if a bid was made) or the free-agent pool (if no bid was made).

Waiver Period

The waiver period can be set by the league manager to 1, 2, 3, or 4 days.

If a player is dropped during the week, the player will be listed on waivers for the set length of total days set in your league rules.

Waivers process on the designated day (e.g., Wednesdays, Thursdays, Fridays, or Saturdays).

FAAB Bidding

Teams can bid on players using their FAAB budget.

The minimum bid is $1, and bids must be in whole dollar increments.

Teams can bid up to their remaining FAAB budget.

Waiver Claims

If multiple teams bid on a player, the claim is resolved based on the FAAB bid amount.

The team with the highest bid will be awarded the player.

Cycling Players

Intentional cycling of players through waivers is a serious offense and may result in a team being banned in Fantasy Football.

A player that is added and released within 24 hours will remain a free agent to prevent cycling.

League Settings

The league manager can change the waiver settings at any time during the season.

To learn more about the waivers process for your league, review your League Settings on the League Office page.

Standard League Waivers

All players not on a fantasy roster reside on waivers following each week of play, remaining there from Tuesday morning until waivers begin to clear at 3 a.m. ET on Thursday.

Released players reside on waivers for at least 48 hours before clearing waivers.

Unclaimed players not acquired during the waiver period become free agents and may be picked up on a first-come, first-serve basis without affecting a team's FAAB budget.

Waiver Rules

Weekly Waivers: Players are on waivers for a set period of time (e.g., 2 days) before clearing waivers.

Continuous Waivers: Players are on waivers until they are claimed or cleared.

No Waivers: Players are not on waivers and can be picked up immediately.
