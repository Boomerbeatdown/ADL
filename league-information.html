League Settings

  • Scoring Rules: The specific scoring system used in the league, including any variations.
  • Roster Settings: The number of starters and bench slots on each team's roster.
  • Draft Type: The type of draft used in the league, such as a snake draft or auction draft.
  • Player Acquisition Method: The method used to acquire players, such as waivers or free agency.
  • Trading Restrictions: Any rules or restrictions on trading players between teams.
  • League Size: The number of teams in the league, which can range from 4 to 18 teams or more.
  • League ID: A unique numeric identifier for the league, used to identify it among all ESPN Fantasy leagues.
